About Me

My eagerness to obtain more knowledge allows me to be driven when it comes to problem solving; never stopping until the goal is achieved! Changing others lives inspires me to be a positive, generous person that gets the job done in the best, most efficient way possible. My professional experience consists of CPR/ First Aid/ Basic Life Support certifications and CNA and Patient Care Technician certifications.
